How to make a sandwich:

  1. First we need to find a good bread, I'd suggest italian ciabatta
  2. Cut the bread on a half and put any sauces of your choice
  3. Bring on that cheese, pecorino does the magic for me
  4. Of course it can't be a sandwich without mortadella
  5. If you want to add a fancy touch, toast it and enjoy it with a nice glass of Aperol Spritz
